# Chapter 16: Launch Angle Transformation (2015-2025)

## Research Question

**How has launch angle evolved, and how did it drive the fly ball revolution?**

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Launch Angle Increased Significantly

| Year | Mean LA | Ground Ball % | Fly Ball % |
|------|---------|---------------|------------|
| 2015 | 11.7° | 48.1% | 22.8% |
| 2025 | 17.8° | 38.1% | 26.3% |
| **Change** | **+6.1°** | **-10.0%** | **+3.5%** |

### 2. Statistical Validation

| Test | Value | Interpretation |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Trend slope | +0.34°/year | Significant increase |
| R² | 0.502 | Substantial fit |
| p-value | 0.015 | Significant |
